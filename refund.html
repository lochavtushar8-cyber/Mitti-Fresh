Refund & Cancellation

Learn about rules for cancelling orders and refund processing timelines for flours and other edible kitchen products.

At Mitti Fresh, we strive to deliver the highest quality food staples. Please read our guidelines regarding order cancellations, hygiene rules, and refund processing timelines.

1. Order Cancellations

You may cancel your order at any time **prior to its dispatch** from our milling facility. To request a cancellation:

  • Please contact us immediately on WhatsApp at 8595077263 with your Order ID.
  • Once an order is marked as *Dispatched* or *Out for Delivery*, we cannot cancel the order.

2. Returns & Hygiene Rules

Since we deal in food staples (stone-ground wheat, multigrain flour, cold-pressed oils, grains, and dosa batters), **we do not accept returns on opened packets or used products** due to safety, health, and hygiene standards.

However, returns or replacements are fully eligible if:

  • The packet was damaged or torn during transit.
  • You received the wrong item or weight variant.

Please share a clear photograph of the damaged or incorrect item with our support team within **24 hours** of delivery to process a replacement or refund.

3. Refund Processing Timelines

For all eligible cancellations or returns, once approved by our auditing team in Dwarka:

  • Approved refunds will take **5-7 business days** to process and transfer back to the customer's original payment method (bank account, card, or UPI VPA).
  • For Cash on Delivery orders, we will request your UPI ID or bank account details to transfer the refund directly within the same timeline.

4. Contact Support

If you have any questions or would like to submit a cancellation/refund request, reach out to our team at:

  • 📍 Production Facility: Dwarka Sector 28, Delhi
  • 📞 WhatsApp Support: 8595077263
  • ✉️ Email Support: support@mittifresh.in
