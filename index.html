Our Heritage

From Kisan Atta Chakki to Mitti Fresh

For years, we served our neighbors as Kisan Atta Chakki, dedicated to quality grain milling. Today, we are taking a leap forward to bring our commitment to unrefined, chemical-free nutrition to a wider audience under our new name: Mitti Fresh.

Our values remain exactly the same. We still believe that healthy living starts with pure ingredients. We extract our oils using traditional, cold-pressed wood pressing (Kohlu) which runs at low speeds and temperatures to guarantee no nutrients are lost. Our grains are stone-ground daily, maintaining the natural goodness of the grain.

Zero Chemical Process

Absolutely no bleach, refining agents, or added chemicals are used during extraction.

Total Transparency

We welcome you to visit our physical store and witness the live milling of your staples.
